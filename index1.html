Alexandre Cassiano

Graduated in Business Management at SENAC, Marketing at Universidade Metodista de São Paulo, Locution at Radioficina and Master of Ceremonies at SENAC. He acts in the locution area since 2008, he has also been working in specific projects of indoor radio (studio in loco and over the internet), voice over advertising and voice over, we attend the national market and also other countries such as Germany and Spain. As master of ceremony, he has experience in the conduction of corporate events, social, sporting and education area.
